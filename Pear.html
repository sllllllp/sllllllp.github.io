    Welcome to Pear Air

    Pear Air is the Irish subsidary of SkyCab Airlines. Pear Air operates flights to and from Ireland. We also connect Ireland to the rest of the world by offering flights to Europe, America and Australia.

    Pear Air is also run by the SkyCab Air Group as we operate as a subsidary to them so the same policies apply.
