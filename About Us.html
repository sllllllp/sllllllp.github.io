All About SkyCab

SkyCab Airlines is a British airline that is based in Belfast International, Northern Ireland. It was set up to be the first proper Northern Irish operating airline as there was no other Northern Irish airline about at that time.

SkyCab is a low-cost airline that flies from the UK to the EU and other destinations around the world. We fly many different types of aircraft to suit the needs of the route and our pilots.

History of SkyCab

SkyCab Airlines was founded on a website called 'ROBLOX'. It was very inactive until the high rank board decided to bring it forward to become a Virtual Airline. SkyCab Virtual was launched on the 1st of January 2019.
